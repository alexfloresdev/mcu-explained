The Marvel Cinematic Universe (often referred to as the MCU) is comprised of 20 interconnected movies (with more on the way), organized into three separate "phases," centered on the stories of over 20 different comic book characters (with more on the way) who originate in the pages of Marvel Comics

While other movies have been made by different movie studios based on comic book characters who also originate in Marvel Comics, the MCU has thus far been the most successful attempt at creating a series of interconnected movies that both tell the individual stories of their characters while simultaneously connecting those stories to a larger narrative that expands across multiple movies

The 20+ movies of the MCU are made by the Disney owned company, Marvel Studios. Marvel Studios is led by current president and all around awesome guy, Kevin Feige.

  • Bruce Banner's disturbing vision causes him to run amuck in a nearby city, and Tony is forced to stop the Hulk using the Hulkbuster armor
  • Due to public outrage over the events in Wakanda, the Avengers go into hiding at Clint Barton's home where they discover that Clint has a wife and two children
  • Thor leaves Clint Barton's home to enlist the help of Dr. Erik Selvig in interpreting his vision. Thor learns that his vision somehow involve the four Infinity Stones seen thus far in the MCU, including the Infinity Stones located within Loki's scepter and the Tesseract
  • While in hiding at Clint's home:
    • Tony and Steve argue over the creation of Ultron
    • Natasha and Bruce consider running away together
    • Nick Fury shows up to help refocus the Avengers in tracking down and stopping Ultron. The Avengers conclude that Ultron is headed to South Korea to enlist Dr. Helen Cho's help in using the Wakandan vibranium to create a new and more perfect body for himself.
  • Already in South Korea, Ultron, Scarlet Witch and Quicksilver are making Dr. Cho use the tissue generating technology to create a new body for Ultron with the regeneration chamber. Beyond just using the built-in tissue-generating material, Ultron adds the Vibranium he purchased in Wakanda and after breaking open the jewel on Loki's scepter, he also implants the Mind Stone that was inside the jewel into the forehead of his currently being formed body
  • While Ultron is uploading his consciousness into the newly formed body, Wanda is able to read Ultron's mind and discovers that Ultron intends to destroy civilization with a home-made meteor. As a result, the Maximoff twins turn against Ultron.
  • Before Ultron is done uploading his consciousness into his new body, Captain America, Hawkeye and Black Widow are successful in taking Ultron's new body away from him. Hawkeye is charged with flying the body back to Avengers Tower
  • After Hawkeye flies the body back to the Avengers Tower, Tony Stark convinces Bruce Banner to take a second try at creating an artificial intelligence but this time using JARVIS as the primary operating system.
