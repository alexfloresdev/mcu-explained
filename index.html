The Marvel Cinematic Universe (often referred to as the MCU) is comprised of 15 interconnected movies (with more on the way), organized into three separate "phases," centered on the stories of over 20 different comic book characters (with more on the way) who originate in the pages of Marvel Comics

While other movies have been made by different movie studios based on comic book characters who also originate in Marvel Comics, the MCU has thus far been the most successful attempt at creating a series of interconnected movies that both tell the individual stories of their characters while simultaneously connecting those stories to a larger narrative that expands across multiple movies

The 15+ movies of the MCU are made by the Disney owned company, Marvel Studios. Marvel Studios is led by current president and all around awesome guy, Kevin Feige

Phase 01

  • Tony Stark aka Iron Man is introduced into the MCU
  • JARVIS is introduced into the MCU
  • Happy Hogan is introduced into the MCU
  • Pepper Potts is introduced into the MCU
  • Colonel James Rhodes is introduced into the MCU (originally played by Terrence Howard)
  • Tony Stark builds a new technology known as the arc reactor to power his first prototype Iron Man suit (also referred to as Mark 1)
  • Agent Coulson is introduced into the MCU
  • S.H.I.E.L.D. is subtly introduced using their full name of the "Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement and Logistics Division"
  • Tony Stark builds the iconic red and gold Iron Man suit (also referred to as Mark 3)
  • Tony Stark announces his identity to the world as "Iron Man"
  • Nick Fury, director of S.H.I.E.L.D. shows up in a post credit sequence as tease for the Avengers
  • Bruce Banner aka The Hulk is introduced into the MCU (originally played by Edward Norton)
  • Col. Rhodes gets an Iron Man Suit of his own and becomes the War Machine
  • Thor's homeworld of Asgard is introduced into the MCU
  • Thor is introduced into the MCU
  • Odin is introduced into the MCU
  • Loki is introduced into the MCU
  • The bifrost, Asgardian technology used to transport individuals across the nine realms is introduced
  • Heimdall, guardian of the bifrost, is introduced into the MCU
  • Lady Sif and the Warriors Three are introduced into the MCU
  • Hawkeye makes a brief cameo and his first appearance in the MCU
  • The bifrost is destroyed
  • Captain America is introduced into the MCU
  • Captain America is given his shield made of a fictional metal known as Vibranium
  • Hawkeye is officially introduced into the MCU
  • Located within the gem of Loki's scepter, the Infinity Stone known as the Mindstone is introduced into the MCU
  • Referred to as the Tesseract, The Infinity Stone known as the Space Stone is introduced into the MCU

Phase 02

  • War Machine is rebranded as the Iron Patriot
  • Tony Stark experiments with remotely controlled Iron Man suits piloted by his artificial intelligence JARVIS
  • The Reality Stone is introduced into the MCU
  • The Collector is briefly introduced into the MCU in a post-credits scene
  • The Reality stone is left in the safekeeping of the Collector
  • Loki fakes his own death and successfully pretends to be Odin and thus ruler of Asgard
  • Sam Wilson aka The Falcon is introduced into the MCU
  • The Winter Soldier is introduced into the MCU
  • Hydra has revealed itself to have infiltrated SHIELD, Captain America is successful in stopping one faction but must join with the Avengers in Avengers Age of Ultron to root out all the other factions
  • The Guardians of the Galaxy are introduced into the MCU which includes Starlord, Groot, Rocket, Drax the Destroyer and Gamora
  • Yondu Udonta is introduced into the MCU
  • Nebula is introduced into the MCU
  • The Power Stone is introduced into the MCU
  • The Collector is officially introduced into the MCU
  • Groot sacrifices his life to save the Guardians
  • Starlord is able to briefly hold the Power Stone, additional help is provided from his fellow guardians
  • Wanda Maximoff aka The Scarlet Witch is introduced into the MCU
  • The Black Widow is able to transform The Hulk back into into Bruce Banner via a "lullaby"
  • Wakanda is briefly introduced into the MCU as a primary source of Vibranium
  • The Hulkbuster armor is introduced into the MCU
  • The Infinity Stone housed within Loki's scepter known as the Mindstone takes center stage in this film and is used to create Wanda's powers as well as the Vision
  • Tony Stark gets a new A.I. powering his suit referred to as Friday
  • The Vision is introduced into the MCU
  • The Avengers relocate from the Avengers tower to a new facility just outside New York
  • The Hulk decides to leave the Avengers and flies off to an unknown location
  • Thor leaves earth in search for the Infinity Stones
  • Ant-Man is introduced into the MCU

Phase 03

  • The King of Wakanda T'chaka is killed in a terrorist bombing for which Bucky Barnes aka the Winter Soldier is implicated as responsible
  • T'challa aka Black Panther is introduced into the MCU
  • Spider-Man is introduced into the MCU
  • Captain America gives up his shield to save Bucky's life and becomes a fugitive
  • In joining with Captain America, Ant-Man, Hawkeye, Scarlet Witch, The Falcon and Black Widow also become fugitives
  • War Machine is badly injured in a confrontation with the Avengers
  • Bucky decides to be frozen again until his Hydra programming can be undone
  • Doctor Strange is introduced into the MCU
  • The concept of a multiverse - many alternate universes and/or dimensions all existing and accessible at any given time - is introduced into the MCU as the source of power used by Doctor Strange, the Ancient One and all other sorcerors in the MCU
  • Master Wong is introduced into the MCU
  • The Cloak of Levitation finds its way to Doctor Strange and functions as his ally
  • The Time Stone is introduced into MCU, known for its abilities to manipulate time
  • Doctor Strange uses the Time Stone to stop Dormammu from consuming earth, this infinity stone is referred to as the Eye of Agamotto among the sorceror community
  • Doctor Strange becomes protector of the Sanctum in New York City and Wong the protector of the Sanctum in China, these two Sanctums provide mystical protection from other-dimensional threats
  • Baby Groot is introduced into the MCU
  • Mantis is introduced into the MCU
  • Starlord is revealed to be the son of a Celestial, hence why he could hold an Infinity Stone in Guardians of the Galaxy
  • Yondu officially becomes a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy
  • Kraglin becomes a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy
  • Mantis becomes a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy
  • Stan Lee's many cameos in the MCU are explained as a result of his involvement with an alien race known as the Watchers
  • Technology from the alien invaders in the first Avengers film is repurposed to make black market weapons
  • Spider-Man involves himself in stopping these weapons from being sold
  • Though offered a role in the Avengers, Peter Parker decides to remain a friendly neighborhood Spider-Man
  • Odin dies
  • Asgard is destroyed and its remaining inhabitants are rescued by Thor and friends
