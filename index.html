The Marvel Cinematic Universe (often referred to as the MCU) is comprised of 20 interconnected movies (with more on the way), organized into three separate "phases," centered on the stories of over 20 different comic book characters (with more on the way) who originate in the pages of Marvel Comics

While other movies have been made by different movie studios based on comic book characters who also originate in Marvel Comics, the MCU has thus far been the most successful attempt at creating a series of interconnected movies that both tell the individual stories of their characters while simultaneously connecting those stories to a larger narrative that expands across multiple movies

The 15+ movies of the MCU are made by the Disney owned company, Marvel Studios. Marvel Studios is led by current president and all around awesome guy, Kevin Feige

  • Pepper Potts is introduced into the MCU

  • Nick Fury, director of S.H.I.E.L.D. shows up in a post credit sequence to propose an idea of the Avengers Initiative

  • Bruce Banner aka The Hulk is introduced into the MCU (originally played by Edward Norton)

  • Bruce Banner is unsuccessful in finding a cure for preventing his Hulk transformation

MCU-Related Events

  • Steve Rogers aka Captain America is introduced into the MCU
  • Hydra, a fictional division of the WWII Nazis, is introduced into the MCU
  • The Tesseract - which is later discovered to be an Infinity Stone known as the Space Stone - is introduced into the MCU
  • Hydra uses the Tesseract to power new technology and new types of weapons, including bombs they intend to drop over major American cities
  • Bucky Barnes - lifelong childhood friend to Steve Rogers - is introduced into the MCU
  • Dr. Arnim Zola - right-hand man of the Red Skull, leader of Hydra - is introduced into the MCU
  • Agent Peggy Carter is introduced into the MCU
  • A young Howard Stark - future father of Tony Stark - is introduced into the MCU
  • Shortly after Steve Rogers becomes Captain America, he saves a unit of soldiers that were captured by Hydra. Bucky Barnes was among this unit of soldiers and as it turns out was being experimented upon by Hydra
  • Captain America is given his shield made of a fictional rare metal known as Vibranium
  • Captain America, along with Bucky Barnes and a select few individuals from the unit he rescued, leads a special taskforce to take down Hydra bases
  • In one of their final missions, Captain America and his squadron are able to capture Dr. Arnim Zola, however Bucky Barnes falls from a great height and is presumed to be dead
  • Informed by Dr. Zola, Captain America and company locate Hydra's final stronghold and arrive just in time to stop Hydra from using a bomber plane to drop bombs over major American cities
  • In the process of stopping the Hydra bomber plane, the Tesseract is lost at sea
  • In order to stop the Hydra bomber plane, Steve Rogers intentionally pilots the plane into the Arctic ocean at the expense of his own life
  • Though the Tesseract is eventually found by Howard Stark, they are unable to find Steve Rogers
  • Steve Rogers wakes up in present day New York City, nearly 70 years after the events of the film have transpired.
  • Shortly after Steve Rogers wakes up, Nick Fury shows up to recruit Steve Rogers for the Avengers

  24. After the dust settles, the Avengers go their separate ways.
  25. Thor uses the Tesseract to return to Asgard along with Loki as his prisoner
  26. In a post-credits scene, Thanos makes his first appearance in the MCU

Universe Events

  • With the bifrost bridge rebuilt, Thor has been traveling across the nine realms fighting alongside Lady Sif and the Warriors Three to restore peace
  • At this same time the Convergence - a rare alignment of the Nine Realms - is approaching. As a result, unusual gravity shifts and portals to different parts of the Nine Realms are happening across the Nine Realms
  • Thor and Jane work together to stop Malekith from using the Aether to plunge the Nine Realms into darkness

  • The Aether encased within a small box is left in the safekeeping of the Collector because the Tesseract is already in safekeeping on Asgard and according to Volstagg and Lady Sif, "it's unwise to keep two Infinity Stones together"

Character Events

  • Loki is brought before Odin to answer for his crimes on Asgard and most recently on earth.
  • Loki is imprisoned on Asgard, where Frigga makes frequent appearances to talk to and console her son.
  • Odin encourages Thor to settle down in Asgard and become the rightful ruler of Asgard
  • When Jane stumbles through a portal and accidentally becomes the host organism for the Aether, Thor brings Jane back to Asgard to receive medical attention
  • With the reappearance of the Aether, an ancient enemy of Thor's grandfather known as Malekith returns to reclaim the Aether with intent to use it to plunge the Nine Realms into darkness
  • Malekith and his army invade Asgard and are unsuccessful in their attempt to reclaim the Aether by kidnapping Jane.
  • Frigga dies protecting Jane from Malekith
  • Even though the Aether hosted within Jane is progressively killing her, Odin orders that Thor not leave Asgard to try and save her while stopping Malekith at the same time
  • Thor breaks Loki out of jail to enlist his help in finding a secret way off of Asgard and execute his plan of simultaneously stopping Malekith and saving Jane
  • Jane is saved, Loki sacrifices his life to save Thor and Malekith escapes with the Aether
  • Thor returns to Asgard, tells Odin of Loki's sacrifice, declines Odin's offer to become ruler of Asgard and decides to return to earth to be with Jane
  • Shortly after Thor leaves Odin's presence, it's revealed to the audience that Loki was using his magic to pretend to be Odin and is therefore the new ruler of Asgard (unbeknownst to Thor). Odin's whereabouts are unknown at this time.

  30. Ultron turns the city of Sokovia into his home-made meteor.   32. Pietro Maximoff sacrifices his life to save Clint Barton's life

  34. The Avengers relocate from the Avengers tower to a new Avengers facility just outside New York City
